===== FULL DESCRIPTION =====

Hangman - Classic Word Guessing Game

The timeless word puzzle you know and love. Guess letters to reveal hidden words before the stick figure is complete.

Features:

• 5 Word Categories
  - Programming terms
  - Animals
  - Countries
  - Movies
  - Food

• Hint System
  Get a clue about the word or reveal a random letter when you're stuck.

• Score Tracking
  Earn points for correct guesses and build winning streaks.

• Offline Play
  Works perfectly without internet connection.

• No Ads
  Pure gameplay with no interruptions.

• Keyboard & Touch Support
  Play with mouse, keyboard, or touchscreen.

How to Play:

1. Choose a category
2. Guess letters by clicking or typing
3. Correct letters appear in the word
4. Wrong guesses add to the stick figure
5. Win by guessing the word before 6 wrong guesses

Perfect for vocabulary building, quick breaks, and family fun.

Privacy: No data collection, no tracking. All game data stays on your device.
