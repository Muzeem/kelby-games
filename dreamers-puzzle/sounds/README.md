# Sound Files Needed

## 1. place.mp3
- Plays when a puzzle piece is correctly placed
- Duration: 0.5-1 second
- Style: Soft chime or gentle click

## 3. celebration.mp3 (NEW)
- Plays when puzzle is completed
- Duration: 2-4 seconds
- Style: Triumphant, uplifting, celebratory
- Examples: Fanfare, success jingle, achievement sound

## Where to Find Celebration Sound

**Free Sound Resources:**
- **Freesound.org** - Search: "success fanfare", "achievement", "victory"
- **Zapsplat.com** - Search: "celebration", "win sound"
- **Mixkit** (mixkit.co) - Success sounds section

**Recommended Style:**
- Uplifting but not childish
- 2-3 seconds duration
- Clear, bright tones
- Matches the elegant tone of the game
