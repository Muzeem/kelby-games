## Free Image Resources

### Public Domain / Creative Commons
- **Unsplash** (unsplash.com) - High-quality, free to use
- **Pexels** (pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- **Pixabay** (pixabay.com) - Free images
- **NASA Image Library** (images.nasa.gov) - All public domain
- **Library of Congress** (loc.gov) - Historical photographs
- **Wikimedia Commons** (commons.wikimedia.org) - Historical images

### Image Optimization Tools
- **TinyPNG** (tinypng.com) - Compress images
- **Squoosh** (squoosh.app) - Google's image optimizer
- **GIMP** (free) or **Photoshop** - Resize to 1200×800px

## Image Guidelines

1. **Resolution:** Exactly 1200×800 pixels
2. **Format:** JPG (for photographs)
3. **File size:** Under 500KB (use compression)
4. **Quality:** 80-85% JPG quality is ideal
5. **Content:** Historically accurate and respectful
6. **Licensing:** Ensure you have rights to use the image
7. **Style:** Clear, recognizable, suitable for puzzle pieces

## Quick Setup

1. Download 7 images matching the themes above
2. Resize each to 1200×800px
3. Compress to under 500KB
4. Rename to match the filenames listed
5. Place in this `images` folder
6. Test the game!
